Problem Description: While construction sites pose serious risk when the human senses are multi-tasking; training in actual live environments is often a necessity for all engineers alike. The construction sites are often noisy, leading to distraction from the delivered content and difficulty in hearing the instructor. Similarly, laboratory experiments require instructions delivered from around the equipment while the instructor/assistant performs the experiment. The inner circles around the equipment limit the outer circles to effectively observe the experiment and/or listen to the instructor.

Using evrywhr can act as the missing part of the puzzle. evrywhr can perform the duty of a third ear when the environmental sounds donâ€™t have to be eliminated completely. In cases, where total insulation from surrounding sound is allowed, noise-cancelling headphones can be used. Where visibility of the instructor/equipment/experiment is the issue, direct vision can be coupled with the device display. evrywhr works on all devices including phones, tablets and desktop.

FAQ

How does evrywhr work?
The speaker decides to broadcast his audio, video or both to either the world, or a chosen group. The world or group members, as the case may be, may choose to listen to shared audio, video or both to enhance their live experience.
Who is evrywhr for?
Primarily aimed to aid instructors in noisy constrained environments, it can be used by everyone who wishes to be present elsewhere. What evrywhr does is enhance your senses, so for example, if your in a trade show and want to check out a few offerings of the competition from your own stand, just hook into their livefeed, and its like you are there. With evrywhr you can be anywhere.
