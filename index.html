Have you wanted to live-stream your audio and video but felt the hassle of configuration stopping you? Evrywhr.me is a no-configuration solution. This means you will want to stream your video all the time.

Choose from the list of sensors, from which you want to allow us the read the data from. And all you NEED to set is a pass-phrase uniquely identifying this session. You need to announce the pass-phrase to your audience so they can join.

Once the session starts, you won't need to have interaction with the system. Unless you decide to close the session, you are locked on to the session. This is to ensure that you can focus on the session. All the settings are handled by us, behind the scenes.

The feature to record and share you session with the world. This ensures later review.

FAQ

How does evrywhr work?
The speaker decides to broadcast his audio, video or both to either the world, or a chosen group. The world or group members, as the case may be, may choose to listen to shared audio, video or both to enhance their live experience.
Who is evrywhr for?
Primarily aimed to aid instructors in noisy constrained environments, it can be used by everyone who wishes to be present elsewhere. What evrywhr does is enhance your senses, so for example, if your in a trade show and want to check out a few offerings of the competition from your own stand, just hook into their livefeed, and its like you are there. With evrywhr you can be anywhere.
